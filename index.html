Coding to Music

One of toughest things for a new front-end web developer is converting layout ideas into HTML and CSS. It seems that for every design challenge, there are dozens of solutions. Each solution comes with additional HTML and CSS properties that need to broken down in order to be fully understood. While this can be frustrating at first, the reward at the end will be the ability to apply a variety of solutions for future design challenges.

I find that listening to music while coding helps keep my mind at ease and prevents me from being distracted while working on tough projects. While everyone has a different taste in music, I find that electronic music with abstract-sounding beats helps me stay focused while working on code.
